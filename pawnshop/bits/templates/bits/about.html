About BITS Pawnshop

A marketplace built by BITSians, for BITSians

Our Story

We’re Vishrut and Anubhooti, two students who wanted to create something useful for the BITS Pilani community. The idea for bits-pilani.store came up during a casual conversation about starting a project that could genuinely help students. That’s when we thought of building a platform where students could easily buy and sell items within campus.
